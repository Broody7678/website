      Apples Are Nutritious and tasty.

      Apples May Be Good for Your Heart.

      They May Have Prebiotic Effects and Promote Good Gut Bacteria.

      Apples Contain Compounds That Can Help Fight Asthma.

  2. Mangoes

    • Mango is the juiciest fruit and it is produced from numerous species of tropical trees. It is known as the king of fruits.
      Scientific name : Mangifera indica.
      Nutrients in mango : Vitamin A, C, E and iron.
    • Benefits of eating mangoes :

      Helps in digestion. Mangoes could help facilitate healthy digestion.
      Promotes Healthy Gut.
      Boosts Immunity.
      Promotes eye health.
      Lowers cholestrol.
      Clears the Skin.
      Aids Weight Loss.

  3. Oranges

    • The orange is the fruit of various citrus species in the family Rutaceae; it primarily refers to Citrus × sinensis, which is also called sweet orange, to distinguish it from the related Citrus × aurantium, referred to as bitter orange.
      Scientific name : Citrus sinensis.
      Nutrients in oranges : Vitamin C, thiamine, folate and potassium.
    • Benefits of eating oranges :

      Healthy immune system.
      Prevents skin damage.
      Keeps blood pressure under check.
      Lowers cholesterol.
      Controls blood sugar level.
      Lowers the risk of cancer.
